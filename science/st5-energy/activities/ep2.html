Energy activity — EP2: Ohm’s Law

Grading

I grade

Get involved in the experiment.

E grade

Get involved in the experiment.

Complete the “E grade” sections of the lab report.

D grade

Do all parts of the experiment.

Complete the “E grade” and “D grade” sections of the lab report.

C grade

Do all parts of the experiment safely.

Complete the “E grade”, “D grade” and “C grade” sections of the lab report.

B grade

Take a leadership role in doing the experiment safely.

Complete the “E grade”, “D grade”, “C grade” and “B grade” sections of the lab report.

A grade

Take a leadership role in doing the experiment safely.

Complete the “E grade”, “D grade”, “C grade”, “B grade” and “A grade” sections of the lab report.
